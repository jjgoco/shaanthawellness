Cookies and analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use this site — which pages get read, roughly where visitors come from, that kind of thing. This only runs if you accept it in the cookie banner. If you reject it or don't respond, no analytics cookies are set. You can change your choice any time via "Cookie settings" in the footer.

Analytics data is processed by Google. We don't use this data for advertising, and we don't sell or share it with anyone else.

Other Google services on this page

Regardless of your cookie choice, this site loads a couple of things directly from Google as part of normal page function:

  • Google Fonts — the typeface used across the site is served from Google's font CDN, which can see the request (including your IP address) the same way any web request does.
  • Google Maps — the map on the home page's "Visit" section is an embedded Google Maps frame.

These aren't cookies we set, and they're not gated by the consent banner — they're part of how the page itself is built. If this matters to you, most browsers let you block third-party embeds directly.

Booking via WhatsApp

Booking happens by messaging us directly on WhatsApp. That conversation is handled by WhatsApp/Meta under their own privacy policy, not ours — we only see what you send us, the same as any WhatsApp chat.

Your choices

  • Clear cookies for this site any time in your browser settings.
